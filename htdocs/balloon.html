Balloon

Overview About Balloon

  • Wall Breakers of the skies, the balloon is a defense-targeting troop that deals massive damage at buildings, but their fragile health can make them weak against air targeting defenses such as the air defense.
  • The balloon is unlocked in town hall 4. They deal damage upon death.
  • They are one of the slowest troops in the game, it is recommended to use a rage or a haste spell when using them for attack.
  • The balloon is one of the best clan castle troops in lower town halls. Due to their small housing space of 5, and the lack of air defenses on town halls 2-4, they dominate the village easily.

Facts:

  • The Balloon is a veteran troop in Clash of Clans. With the Barbarian, Archer, Goblin, Giant, Wall Breaker, Wizard, Healer, Dragon, and PEKKA, they were in the game upon its release.
  • The damage they deal when they die is similar to their bomb damage, except if the balloon's levels are 7-10.
  • Balloons don't actually attack air troops. This is because their bombs simply explode on the ground, which makes them only target ground.
  • Similar to the wall breaker, the balloon attacks the nearest building/troop/hero after all the defenses are gone.
  • The balloon's first attack is faster than the preceding attacks. Ths might be due for faster connections to buildings due to their slow movement.
  • The Balloons are at Clash Royale as well, a tanky and a powerful card.
